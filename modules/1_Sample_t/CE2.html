Fecal Coliform Counts II

Students recorded fecal coliform counts (number of colony forming units (CFU) per 100 ml) in water samples from a nearby lake following a rainstorm with considerable runoff. The results from their samples are shown below.

     n nvalid   mean     sd    min     Q1 median     Q3    max 
 25.00  25.00 168.19  22.20 112.60 151.40 170.50 179.20 208.80

Test, at the 1% level, if these results differ from the mean of 162 CFU/100ml predicted from a computer model. Make sure to provide explicit answers with your work shown for all 11 steps of a hypothesis test.
