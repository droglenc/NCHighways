Where are the data files that Prof. Ogle provides for us?

Many times I will refer to data files that I have made available to you or, in the text, it will mention a file name with a ".txt" extension. These files are available from the Local Downloadable Data Files link on the R Resources webpage. These files can be downloaded by right-clicking on them and saving them to your disk. They are then loaded into R with read.table().
