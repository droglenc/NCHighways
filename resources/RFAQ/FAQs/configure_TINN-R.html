How should I configure TINN-R?

The TINN-R applications in the Northland computer labs have been configured to match my preferences for using this application. You can match these preferences with the following steps.

\ \
  1. Check that Tinn-R and R are communicating properly.
    • If there is an R icon on the toolbar with a green arrow in it (see right (top)) then communication is proper.
    • If communication is not proper then choose the "R" main menu, "Configure" submenu, and "Permanent (Rprofile.site)" subusubmenu.
  1. Turn on word wrapping. --
    • Choose the "options" main menu and "application" submenu.
    • In the ensuring dialog box (right) select the "main" tab and the "general" subtab.
    • Click on the fifth check box to turn on the "word wrapping" (i.e., should now be checked).
  1. Have TINN-R and R organized automatically. --
    • Choose the "options" main menu and "application" submenu.
    • Choose the "R" tab, "Rgui" subtab, and "options" subsubtab.
    • Click on the "Organize automatically" check box (i.e., should now be checked).
  1. Show line numbers with leading zeroes. --
    • Choose the "options" main menu and "editor" submenu.
    • In the "Display" tab click on the "show line numbers" and "show leading zeroes" check boxes (i.e., should now be checked).
  1. [OPTIONAL] Turn off active line yellow highlighting. --
    • Choose the "options" main menu and "Colors Preferences" submenu.
    • Click off the "Active line (choice)" check box (should be unchecked).

  1. [OPTIONAL] Turn on (or off is shown in dialog box) return focus after submitting. --
    • Choose the "options" main menu and "return focus to editor" submenu.
  1. [OPTIONAL] Create a hot-key (F4) for submitting a line. --
    • Choose the "R" main menu and the "Hot keys (operational system)" submenu.
    • Click on "Send line" option in "Send and Control" box (eleventh item in list).
    • Click cursor in "Set (hotkey)" box.
    • Press the F4 key (first FX key available for use).
    • Press "Add" button.
    • Make sure that "Active" radio button is selected.
    • Press OK
