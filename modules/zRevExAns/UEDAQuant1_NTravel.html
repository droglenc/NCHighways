The calculation of the mean and standard deviation is shown below.

Demonstration of parts of a std. dev. calculation.

     x diffs diffs.sq
1    8    -3        9
2    3    -8       64
3   17     6       36
4   15     4       16
5    4    -7       49
6   19     8       64
sum 66     0      238

Mean = x-bar = 66 / 6 = 11 

Variance = s^2 = 238 / 5 = 47.6 

Std. Dev = s = sqrt(47.6) = 6.899275
