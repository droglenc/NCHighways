I accidentally saved the workspace image. How do I remove it?

Upon closing R you may be asked to save the workspace image. In your classes at Northland it is best to say "No" to this query. If you accidentally save the workspace image you will get the following message when opening R in the future.

[previously saved workspace restored]

The easiest way to remove this restored workspace is to use unlink() in the following way,

> unlink(".RData")

You can then close R and re-open it. The saved workspace should not be restored.
