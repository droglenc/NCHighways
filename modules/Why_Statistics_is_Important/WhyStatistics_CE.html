The following code is used to produce a “live” example for class. The meaning of this code will be explained later. For now, we simply want the results that are produced. Thus, type the code exactly as shown here.

Job 1 – Load the NCStats package

> library(NCStats)

Job 2 – Obtain a Random Sample of Residents from Ashland-Bayfield Counties

> data(ABCens90)
> ( smp1 <- srsdf(ABCens90,50) )
  • Were the individuals in your sample identical? Why or why not?
  • Can you quickly explain the “general” age and sex of individuals in your sample? Why or why not?

Job 3 – Summarize that Sample

> Summarize(~age,data=smp1,digits=1)
> Summarize(~sex,data=smp1)
  • Can you quickly explain the “general” age and sex of individuals in your sample? Why or why not?

Job 4 – Obtain a Second Random Sample of Residents from Ashland-Bayfield Counties

> ( smp2 <- srsdf(ABCens90,50) )
> Summarize(~age,data=smp2,digits=1)
> Summarize(~sex,data=smp2)
  • Were the mean ages from the two samples identical?
  • Were the percentage of females from the two samples identical?
  • Are your results typical or expected? Why or why not?
