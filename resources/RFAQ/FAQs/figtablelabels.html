How should I label and refer to tables and figures?

Most scientific journals label figures and tables with a unique number followed by a descriptive caption and then refer to that figure or table number in the text where appropriate. Figure labels and captions go UNDERNEATH the figure and table lables and caption go ABOVE the table. Remember -- "table top".
