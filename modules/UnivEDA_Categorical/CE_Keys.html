Popularity in Middle School

  1. The goal for most kids was getting good grades, followed by being popular, and then being good in sports. 
    2. > d <- read.csv("PopularKids.csv")
    > ( tbl <- xtabs(~goals,data=d) )
    goals
 Grades Popular  Sports 
    247     141      90 
    > barplot(tbl,xlab="Student's Goal",ylab="Frequency",ylim=c(0,250))
  2. Students feel that “being good in sports” is an important contributor to popularity as approximately 2/3rds of studens ranked this as either “most important” or “somewhat important” to being popular. 
    3. > tbl <- xtabs(~sports,data=d)
> percTable(tbl,digits=1)
    sports
   1    2    3    4  Sum 
34.5 32.2 23.4  9.8 99.9 
    > barplot(tbl,xlab="Rank of Sports in Popularity",ylab="Frequency",ylim=c(0,200))
